Million Dollar Chicken Casserole

Read here for the recipe

This recipe is sure to make an impression on both your friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon Creole seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 5 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 1 teaspoon sliced scallions

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Stir together cream of chicken soup, cottage cheese, sour cream, creole seasoning, onion power and the garlic in a bowl.
  3. Smooth chicken mixture evenly into an 11x7 inch pan and sprinkle evenly with mozzarella.
  4. Place on a foil-linked baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes.
