World's Best Lasagna

Read here for the recipe

This recipe combines all the wonderful things in the world. Pasta and love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloes garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozarrella cheese, sliced

Steps

  1. In an oven, cool sausage, beef, onion and garlic over medium heat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds and Italian seasoning.
  4. Simmer covered for about 1 and a half hours, stirring occasionally.
